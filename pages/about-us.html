Our Story

IMG Tool.in was born from a simple need. As digital creators ourselves, we understood how crucial—and often tedious—it was to optimize images for websites. We wanted a tool that was fast, free, and easy to use, without compromising on privacy. When we couldn't find exactly what we were looking for, we decided to build it ourselves.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower creators, developers, and marketers with simple, powerful, and accessible tools to make the web a faster and more efficient place. We believe in privacy, performance, and providing value without barriers.

Why Choose Us?

  • Performance-Focused: Our tools are designed to help you improve your website's speed and Core Web Vitals.
  • Your Privacy is Our Priority: Files are deleted after processing. We minimize what we store and only for short, necessary intervals when required for a tool.
  • Simple and Powerful: Get professional results without navigating complex settings.
  • Free for Everyone: Use our core tools without hidden costs, watermarks, or limitations.
