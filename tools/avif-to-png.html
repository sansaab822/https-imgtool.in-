Convert AVIF to PNG with Transparency

While AVIF is excellent for web compression, PNG (Portable Network Graphics) remains the standard for lossless quality and transparency. Whether you are a designer needing assets for Photoshop or just want a format that works everywhere, our tool bridges the gap.

Why choose PNG over AVIF?

  • Software Support: Almost all image editors (Photoshop, Canva, GIMP) fully support PNG, unlike AVIF.
  • Lossless Editing: If you plan to edit the image further, PNG prevents generation loss.
  • Alpha Channel: PNG offers superior handling of semi-transparent pixels compared to many other formats.

How to use:

  1. Upload your .avif files.
  2. Ensure the "Preserve Transparency" toggle is ON (it is by default).
  3. Click Convert and wait a moment.
  4. Download your high-quality PNGs.

Common Questions

Will the file size increase?

Yes, typically. PNG is a lossless format, so files are often larger than highly compressed AVIFs, but the quality is perfect.
