Frequently Asked Questions

Will my PNG's transparency be preserved?
Yes, both PNG and GIF support transparency. Our tool maintains the transparency from your original PNG files in the output GIF, though GIF uses binary transparency (pixels are either fully transparent or fully opaque).
How do I create an animated GIF?
Simply upload two or more PNG images. The tool will automatically combine them into an animated GIF. You can control the animation speed using the frame delay option.
What is the color limit for GIFs?
GIF format is limited to 256 colors per frame. For images with many colors like photographs, some color detail may be lost. GIF is best suited for graphics, logos, and simple animations.
