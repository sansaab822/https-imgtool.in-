Why Convert AVIF to WebP?

While AVIF offers incredible compression, WebP is currently the king of web compatibility. Developed by Google, WebP images load instantly on almost every device and browser, making them the perfect choice for faster websites and better SEO.

WebP Advantages:

  • Universal Support: Works flawlessly on older Android phones and browser versions where AVIF might fail.
  • Faster Decoding: WebP images decode quicker than AVIF on mobile devices, saving battery life.
  • Best of Both Worlds: You get the transparency of PNG and the small size of JPG in one format.

Common Questions

Is WebP better than AVIF?

For raw compression, AVIF wins. But for compatibility and speed, WebP is currently the better choice for live websites.

Do I need to upload files?

No! Our converter runs entirely in your browser. Your images never leave your computer.
