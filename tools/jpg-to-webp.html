About JPG to WebP Conversion

Last Updated:

JPG is a classic format for photos, but WebP is a modern image format developed by Google that provides superior compression for images on the web. Converting from JPG to WebP can significantly reduce file sizes without sacrificing quality, leading to faster website load times and improved user experience.

How to Convert JPG to WebP

  2. Adjust the optional settings like 'Quality' or enable 'Lossless Compression' if needed.
  3. Once your files are ready, click the 'Convert to WebP' button.
  4. After the conversion is complete, download your new, optimized WebP files.

Why Convert From JPG to WebP?

  • Superior Compression: WebP's advanced algorithms create much smaller files than JPG for the same level of quality.
  • Lossy & Lossless: WebP supports both lossy (like JPG) and lossless (like PNG) compression in a single format. It even supports transparency and animation.
  • Web Performance: Smaller images mean faster page loads, which is crucial for SEO and keeping visitors engaged.
