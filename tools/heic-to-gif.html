Yes. When you upload a HEIC file containing a Live Photo (an image sequence), our tool converts that sequence into an animated GIF, preserving the motion and creating a continuous loop. For standard, static HEIC photos, it creates a single-frame GIF.
Will there be quality loss when converting from HEIC to GIF?
The GIF format has a 256-color limitation, whereas HEIC supports a much wider color range. For photos with complex gradients and many colors, you may notice color depth reduction. However, for most Live Photos and simple graphics, the conversion looks great and preserves the animation perfectly.
