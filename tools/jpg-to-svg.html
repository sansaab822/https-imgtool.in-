Frequently Asked Questions

Are my files secure?
Yes, 100% secure. This tool processes all images directly in your web browser. Your files are never uploaded to our servers.
Why is my SVG file larger/smaller than the original JPG?
SVG file size depends on the complexity of vector paths, not pixels. Simple images like logos often create smaller SVGs, while complex photos can produce very large files due to the high number of paths.
What is the benefit of this vectorizer?
Unlike simple format converters, this tool rebuilds your image using vector shapes. This makes it infinitely scalable, perfect for high-resolution printing and web design, where quality is critical.
