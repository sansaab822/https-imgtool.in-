About JPG to AVIF Conversion

Last Updated:

AVIF (AV1 Image File Format) is a cutting-edge image format offering massive improvements in compression efficiency over older formats like JPG and even WebP. Converting your JPGs to AVIF can dramatically reduce file sizes, which is critical for building fast, modern websites.

How to Convert JPG to AVIF

  1. Click the 'Select Files' button or drag and drop your JPG files.
  2. Adjust the 'Quality' slider to balance file size and visual quality.
  3. Click the 'Convert to AVIF' button.
  4. Download your new, highly optimized AVIF files.

Why Convert From JPG to AVIF?

  • Incredible Compression: AVIF can compress images to a fraction of the size of a JPG with comparable visual quality, often saving over 50%.
  • Modern Features: It supports high dynamic range (HDR), 12-bit color depth, and both lossy and lossless compression.
  • Royalty-Free: AVIF is an open, royalty-free format backed by major tech companies like Google, Apple, and Microsoft.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does converting from JPG to AVIF reduce quality?
AVIF uses 'lossy' compression by default. A lower quality setting will result in a smaller file but may introduce artifacts. We recommend a quality level around 50-60 as a great starting point.
Is this JPG to AVIF converter free?
Yes, absolutely! Our converter is 100% free to use with full access to the quality control options.
Are my uploaded files secure?
Your privacy is paramount. All conversions happen directly in your browser. Your images are never uploaded to our servers.
What makes AVIF better than WebP or JPG?
AVIF offers even better compression than WebP and JPG, meaning it can create even smaller files for the same quality. It also supports modern features like HDR and 12-bit color.
Is AVIF ready for production websites?
Yes, AVIF is supported by all major modern browsers (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge) and is being adopted by major tech companies, making it a future-proof choice.
