The Best Way to Convert AVIF to JPG

AVIF is a modern image format that offers superior compression, but it's not yet supported by every browser or software. Our AVIF to JPG converter solves this problem by instantly transforming your images into the universal JPEG format.

Why use this tool?

  • Universal Compatibility: JPG opens on any device, phone, or old computer.
  • Social Media Ready: Instagram, Facebook, and Twitter prefer JPG files.
  • No Watermarks: Get clean, professional images without branding.
  • Preserve Transparency: We automatically fill transparent backgrounds with white (or your custom color) since JPG doesn't support transparency.

How to Convert:

  1. Upload your .avif files to the box above.
  2. Adjust the Quality slider if you need smaller file sizes.
  3. Click Convert All.
  4. Download your new JPGs individually or as a ZIP file.
